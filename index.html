Protecting Heirloom Seeds for Future Generations

🌱 About

The Bohol Heirloom Seed Conservation Network is a provincial initiative dedicated to preserving traditional vegetable seeds and strengthening local farming systems.

🎯 Mission

To conserve agricultural biodiversity, improve food security, and empower farmers through community-based seed conservation and training.

🌾 Programs

  • Heirloom Seed Conservation
  • Farmer Training & Workshops
  • Community Seed Banking
  • School & Youth Gardening

🏛 Municipal Membership

Bronze: 1 training seminar per year

Silver: Seminar + demonstration farm

Gold: Full seed bank + custodians + annual exchange
