Solanaceae Strain

Native Varieties (Heat Armor)

Ultra-resilient nightshades selected for exceptional heat tolerance and natural pest barriers. Featuring robust stalk profiles and thickened cuticles, these native landrace peppers, eggplants, and tomatoes are bred to withstand extreme weather shocks while delivering heavy, high-flavor yields without intensive chemical inputs.

Leguminosae Strain

Indigenous Legumes (Living Nitrogen)

High-vigor heirloom field beans and climbing varieties optimized for vertical polyculture. These indigenous legumes feature hyper-efficient root-nodule symbiosis, pulling atmospheric nitrogen down to naturally feed your soil while maximizing vertical space with aggressive vining habits and heavy, protein-rich yields.

Cucurbitaceae Strain

Traditional Cucurbits (Living Mulch)

Traditional, deep-rooted squashes and melons engineered for total ground-cover dominance. Their massive fan leaves form a living canopy that traps soil moisture and suppresses weeds, while extensive taproot networks lock in drought-hardy vigor and concentrate rich, ancient flavor profiles into every fruit.
